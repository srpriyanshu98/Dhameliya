About Us

Dhameliya Kidney Hospital has been born out of the need for a good center which catered to patients with complicated kidney diseases in Surat. It is a judicious blend of high-end technology, world-class expertise, and outstanding personalized care. With time, it has become the best kidney hospital in Surat.
